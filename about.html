About Toad Town Services

Your Northern Wisconsin Partner for Home Services and Technology Solutions

Who We Are

Toad Town Services brings together technical expertise and hands-on problem-solving to serve homeowners and small organizations throughout northern Wisconsin. We handle everything from IT support and audio/streaming systems to general handyman work and home technology integration.

Our Story

After spending years in data analytics and corporate IT, I realized my real passion was solving practical problems for real people in my community. Growing up with a technical background—from broadcast engineering certification to IT networking and hands-on manufacturing experience—I've always been the person who figures out how things work and how to fix them.

I've been volunteering with my church's audio and streaming setup, helping friends and neighbors with their technology challenges, and tackling complex renovation projects on my own 1960s split-level home. These experiences showed me there's a real need in our area for someone who can bridge the gap between technical knowledge and practical service—someone who can troubleshoot your network, hang drywall, set up your streaming system, or diagnose why your smart home devices won't connect.

Toad Town Services is built on that foundation: combining diverse skills to help people maintain and improve their homes and navigate the technology that's increasingly part of daily life.

Why Choose Us

Diverse Skill Set: Our background spans IT, broadcast engineering, audio systems, construction, and hands-on problem-solving. We understand how systems work together and can tackle problems that cross traditional service boundaries.

Real-World Experience: We're not just reading manuals—we're actively working on the same kinds of projects in our own home, staying current with both technology and practical building challenges.

Patient, Educational Approach: We take time to explain what we're doing and why, so you understand your systems and feel confident managing them.

Local Understanding: We know northern Wisconsin—the challenges of rural connectivity, seasonal issues, and the practical realities of maintaining homes and technology in our climate.

Honest Communication: Clear estimates, transparent pricing, and straightforward advice about what you actually need versus what's optional.

Our Commitment

We approach every project—whether it's setting up a church's streaming system, troubleshooting a home network, or helping with a renovation challenge—with the same attention to detail and commitment to quality. We're building a business based on doing good work, treating people fairly, and being a reliable resource for our community.

We're here to help you solve problems, improve your home, and make technology work the way it should.

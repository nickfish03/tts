About Toad Town Services

Your local IT support and audio engineering specialist

Who We Are

Toad Town Services is your trusted local partner for IT services and audio engineering in Oneida County, Wisconsin. We understand that technology should work for you, not against you.

Our Story

Born and raised in the Northwoods, I've always been the person friends and neighbors call when something needs fixing. Whether it's a computer that won't start, helping someone set up their new smartphone, or mixing sound for a church service, I genuinely enjoy solving problems and helping people.

After years of helping friends, family, and local churches with technology and audio needs, I decided to turn my passion into a business to serve our entire community. I believe in honest work, fair prices, and taking the time to explain what I'm doing so you understand your technology better.

Why Choose Us

Local Knowledge: We know the unique challenges of living in northern Wisconsin - from rural internet connectivity to helping folks stay connected with technology.

Patient Approach: Especially with technology support, we take the time to teach, not just fix. We want you to feel confident using your devices.

Honest Pricing: No hidden fees, no inflated charges. We provide clear estimates and explain all costs upfront.

Reliable Service: When we say we'll be there, we show up. Your time is valuable, and we respect that.

Our Promise

Every job we take on - whether it's optimizing your church's sound system or helping you video call your grandchildren - gets our full attention. We're not happy until you're happy, and we stand behind all our work.

We're proud to be part of the Oneida County community and look forward to helping you with whatever technology or audio challenges come your way.
